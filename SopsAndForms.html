Institutional Review Board



Standard Operating Procedures and Forms


This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) intends to describe the Institutional Review Board (TMC-IRB), its function, composition, membership requirements, and process of IRB recruitment and terms of reference for the committee members who perform the technical and ethical review of biomedical and socio-behavioral research in The Medical City (TMC).
